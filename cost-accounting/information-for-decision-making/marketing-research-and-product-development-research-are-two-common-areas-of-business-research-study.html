Marketing research and product development research are two common areas of business research study.

(A) ** True

(B) False

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -It provides a detailed analysis of how efficiently business management and marketing strategies are working and the scope for improvement. There are two main types of business research corresponding to quantitative analysis and qualitative analysis.

Concept note-2: -Marketing research and product development research are two common areas of business research study. Managers should strive for consistency and objectivity in their work.

Concept note-3: -Which of the following statements is true about the information research process? It is a systematic approach to collecting, analyzing, interpreting, and transforming data into decision-making information.

Concept note-4: -Which of the following is NOT a common activity of a marketing research department? Creating new advertising.
