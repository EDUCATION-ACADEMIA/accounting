The following data relates to the financial position of Whittaker plc for the 2016-2017 financial year. * Revenue:£25 750 million * Dividends paid:£2 655 million * Gross profit margin:24.5% * Net profit margin:5.6%Whittaker plc’s gross profit for the 2015-2016 financial year was:

(A) £1 442.00 million

(B) £148.68 million

(C) £650.48 million

(D) ** £6 308.75 million

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -An Income Statement is traditionally used to measure profitability of the business for the past accounting period.

Concept note-2: -Profitability ratios are financial metrics used by analysts and investors to measure and evaluate the ability of a company to generate income (profit) relative to revenue, balance sheet assets, operating costs, and shareholders’ equity during a specific period of time.

Concept note-3: -Net profit margin It is the revenue that is left over after all the company’s operating and non-operating costs have been paid. The only outgoing figures that are not included are dividends paid to investors, as these are not classed as an expense.

Concept note-4: -4.9. Hypothesis 2 (H2): profitability is proven to positively affect the stock price variable, meaning that the higher the company generates profits, the higher the stock price in the capital market.
