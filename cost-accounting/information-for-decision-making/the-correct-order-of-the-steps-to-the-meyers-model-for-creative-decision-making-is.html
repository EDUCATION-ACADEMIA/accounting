The correct order of the steps to the Meyers Model for Creative Decision Making is

(A) Problem finding, fact finding, idea finding, recognition, acceptance finding, solution finding

(B) Problem finding, fact finding, recognition, idea finding, solution finding, acceptance finding.

(C) ** Recognition, fact finding, problem finding, idea finding, solution finding, acceptance finding

(D) Recognition, problem finding, fact finding, idea finding, solution finding and acceptance finding

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Creative decision-making utilizes a five-step process. Steps to using the creative decision-making model include understanding the problem, learning all relevant information, brainstorming new possibilities, reviewing ideas, and implementing the change.

Concept note-2: -The four steps to the creative process are: preparation, incubation, illumination, and implementation.

Concept note-3: -problem-solving (CPS) process, is (1) Mess finding, (2) Fact finding, (3) Problem finding, (4) Idea finding, (5) Solution finding, and (6) Acceptance finding.
