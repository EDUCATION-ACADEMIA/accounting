____ are the fundamental guidelines for the decisions and actions of managers.

(A) Controlling

(B) Executives

(C) ** Management Principles

(D) Planning

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -True. Principles of management are statements of fundamental truths, which serve as guidelines for decisions and actions of managers.

Concept note-2: -The application of principles of management helps the managers to take right decisions at the right time. These principles of management help managers to tackle the diverse problems in a dynamic business environment.

Concept note-3: -Management principles provide general guidelines in tackling the organizational situations wisely as well as in solving the problems. They are not rigid. Which management principles are to be applied depends upon the situation, size, and nature of the organization.

Concept note-4: -Principles of management help managers in taking decisions scientifically as the knowledge of management principles enables managers to learn the cause and effect relationship between variables operating in the organisation.

Concept note-5: -At the most fundamental level, management is a discipline that consists of a set of five general functions: planning, organizing, staffing, leading and controlling.
