The process of talking to other people about their jobs is called:

(A) mentoring

(B) pestering

(C) shadowing

(D) ** networking

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Networking is the process of making connections and building relationships. These connections can provide you with advice and contacts, which can help you make informed career decisions. Networking can even help you nd unadvertised jobs/internships.

Concept note-2: -Networking, also known as computer networking, is the practice of transporting and exchanging data between nodes over a shared medium in an information system.

Concept note-3: -Network engineers set up, develop and maintain computer networks within an organisation or between organisations. You’ll offer support to users, who can be staff, clients, customers and suppliers and troubleshoot any problems.

Concept note-4: -What are examples of networking? Examples of networking include going to a business conference and chatting with likeminded individuals, meeting up with a former colleague for lunch, emailing a client to wish them a happy birthday, and having an informal brainstorming session with coworkers at the end of each week.
