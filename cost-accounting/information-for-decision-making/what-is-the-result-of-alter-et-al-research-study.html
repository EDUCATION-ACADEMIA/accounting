What is the result of Alter et al. Research study?

(A) Participants answered less questions correctly when the font was difficult to read.

(B) Participants answered no questions correctly when the font was difficult to read.

(C) ** Participants answered more questions correctly when the font was difficult to read.

(D) None of the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The results suggested that participants answered more questions correctly when the font was difficult to read. The big idea is that something as minor as disfluency is enough to trigger rational thinking. The hard-to-read font works like a cue or signal that triggers more System 2 thinking about the problem.

Concept note-2: -Longitudinal study So, once again, researchers do not interfere with their subjects. However, in a longitudinal study, researchers conduct several observations of the same subjects over a period of time, sometimes lasting many years.

Concept note-3: -Researchers use experiments rather than other research methods in order to distinguish between: causes and effects.
