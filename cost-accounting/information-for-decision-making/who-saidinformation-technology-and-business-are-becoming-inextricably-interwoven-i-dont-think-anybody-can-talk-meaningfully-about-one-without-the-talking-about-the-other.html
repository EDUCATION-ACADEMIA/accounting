Who said:Information technology and business are becoming inextricably interwoven. I don’t think anybody can talk meaningfully about one without the talking about the other.

(A) Jeff Bezos

(B) Elon Musk

(C) ** Bill Gates

(D) Karren Brady

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Information technology and business are becoming inextricably interwoven. I don’t think anybody can talk meaningfully about one without the talking about the other.

Concept note-2: -“It’s fine to celebrate success, but it is more important to heed the lessons of failure.” It’s easy to get caught up in the excitement of success, but success on its own has very little to teach you. It’s important to pay attention to your failures and learn from them going forward.

Concept note-3: -"Success is a lousy teacher. It seduces smart people into thinking they can’t lose.” The takeaway: Doing well and thriving is what you’ve been aiming for all your life, so congratulations on getting to this point.

Concept note-4: -A famous quote of Bill Gates, “If your business is not on the Internet then your business will be out of business” is undeniably the adhering truth of our time.

Concept note-5: -Leadership Advice Quotes by Bill Gates “As we look ahead into the next century, leaders will be those who empower others.” “If your culture doesn’t like geeks, you are in real trouble.” “Life is not fair; get used to it.” “To win big, you sometimes have to take big risks.”
