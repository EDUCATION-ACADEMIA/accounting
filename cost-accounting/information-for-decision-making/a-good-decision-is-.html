(A) ** well thought out

(B) made quickly

Concept note-1: -Definition of ‘well-thought-out’ One’s got to have a well-thought-out idea how to cover, say, the first five miles. A well-thought-out plan is needed to counter some unfair publicity. You can turn hopes and wishes into a well-thought-out action plan.

Concept note-2: -adjective. : carefully considered and formed.
