EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Step 1: Identify the decision You realize that you need to make a decision. Try to clearly define the nature of the decision you must make. This first step is very important.

Concept note-2: -Selecting the best solution is the most critical part of decision making. There is a risk element involved in each alternative against the expected gain. Selection should be made at proper time.

Concept note-3: -Weigh the evidence. Choose among the alternatives. Take action. Review your decision.
