Selecting a set of brands and then considering when and where to buy describes which of the following types of purchase behavior?

(A) ** Item first

(B) Outlet first

(C) Function first

(D) Price first

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Habitual buying behavior This type of consumer buying behavior is characterized by low involvement in a purchase decision. A client sees no significant difference among brands and buys habitual goods over a long period. An example of habitual buying behavior is purchasing everyday products.

Concept note-2: -1. Problem recognition. The first step of the consumer decision-making process is recognizing the need for a service or product.

Concept note-3: -Buying Behavior is the decision processes and acts of people involved in buying and using products. Need to understand: why consumers make the purchases that they make? what factors influence consumer purchases? the changing factors in our society.
