Which term is used to identify the tasks to be done, employees assigned to the work, and the time frame for completion of each task?

(A) work calendar

(B) work flowchart

(C) work outline

(D) ** work schedule

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A Schedule of Works is a contract document that lists the works required on a project. The Schedule references the requirements included in the specifications and contract drawings plus any additional ‘builders work’ or ‘fixing schedule’ type items, this is commonly referred to as a specified schedule of work.

Concept note-2: -Time management is the process of organizing and planning how to divide your time between different activities. Get it right, and you’ll end up working smarter, not harder, to get more done in less time – even when time is tight and pressures are high.

Concept note-3: -Employee scheduling is important to all businesses, big or small. A manager or a supervisor creates work schedule in small businesses. In big organizations, employee scheduling is done by the HR management.
