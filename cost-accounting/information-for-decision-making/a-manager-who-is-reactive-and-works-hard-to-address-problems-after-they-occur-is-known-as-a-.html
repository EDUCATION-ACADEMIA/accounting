A manager who is reactive and works hard to address problems after they occur is known as a ____

(A) problem seeker

(B) ** problem solver

(C) problem avoider

(D) problem manager

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Reacting to a problem with intensity and being dedicated to finding alternatives to resolve the issue makes them a problem solver.

Concept note-2: -1. Rational: The rational decision-making model is logical and approaches the problem in a linear fashion, step-by-step, to eliminate uncertainty and emotion from the decision-making process.

Concept note-3: -Managers who are problem seekers are always looking for problems to solve or opportunities to explore. True problem seekers are proactive as information gatherers, and they are forward thinking.

Concept note-4: -The correct answer is a) systematic thinker. A systematic thinker uses a rational approach to conclude the problem. Such individuals consider all factors before proposing a conclusion.
