An organizational objective is to implement the Occupational Health System and an indicator that helps us measure the results is:

(A) unproductive times

(B) ** Work accident

(C) evaluation index

(D) Compliance with the training plan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -An occupational health indicator is a measure of a work-related disease or injury, or a factor associated with occupational health, such as workplace exposures, hazards, or interventions, in a workforce population.

Concept note-2: -The objectives are to achieve: Continuous reduction in the incidence of work related injuries, fatalities, diseases, disasters and loss of national assets.
