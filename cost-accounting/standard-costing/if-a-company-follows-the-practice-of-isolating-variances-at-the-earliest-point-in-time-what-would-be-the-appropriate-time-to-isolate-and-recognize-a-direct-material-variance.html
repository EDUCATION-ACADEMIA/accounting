If a company follows the practice of isolating variances at the earliest point in time, what would be the appropriate time to isolate and recognize a direct material variance?

(A) when material is issued

(B) ** when material is purchased

(C) when material is used in production

(D) when purchase order is originated

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct answer is b. When the material is purchased. At the time of purchase of direct materials, information about the actual quantity and the actual price is first available. So, direct material price variance is firstly recognized at that point in time.

Concept note-2: -C. If the actual labor hours worked exceeded standard hours allowed is expressed as unfavorable labor efficiency variance.

Concept note-3: -Unfavourable labour rate variance.

Concept note-4: -10-8 The materials price variance is usually the responsibility of the purchasing manager. The materials quantity and labor efficiency variances are usually the responsibility of production managers and supervisors.

Concept note-5: -What type of direct material variances for price and quantity will arise if the actual number of pounds of materials used exceeds standard pounds allowed but actual cost was less than standard cost? Labor rate variance.
