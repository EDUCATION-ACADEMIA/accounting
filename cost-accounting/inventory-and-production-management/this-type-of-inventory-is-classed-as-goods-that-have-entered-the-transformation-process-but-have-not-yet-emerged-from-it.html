This type of inventory is classed as goods that have entered the transformation process but have not yet emerged from it.

(A) Buffer Stock

(B) Pipeline inventory

(C) Finished goods inventory

(D) ** Work in progress inventory

(E) Anticipation inventory

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -There are four main types of inventory: raw materials/components, WIP, finished goods and MRO.

Concept note-2: -Indirect raw materials are the components that are not part of the final products but are used during the production process.

Concept note-3: -Work-in-progress inventory is the partially finished goods waiting for completion and resale. WIP inventory is also known as inventory on the production floor.

Concept note-4: -Work-In-Process Inventory includes all the direct materials, direct labor and manufacturing overhead costs that have been added to the manufacturing process but for which production has not been completed.
