Planning purchases may also include ____

(A) Never changing your inventory

(B) Not planning purchases

(C) ** Making changes to inventory based on needs for upcoming events or other things

(D) Keeping inventory the same because it’s easier to manage it that way.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Inventory planning helps companies buy the right amount of stock and decide how often to reorder. Inventory planning helps lower the costs of keeping items in stock and helps make sure there is enough stock for making and selling items.

Concept note-2: -Inventory planning is a process for deciding what retail products to stock and how much to order. The practice involves demand forecasting and budgeting, in which retailers factor in sales data and trends to determine the optimal inventory levels and the right timing for sales, promotions, and markdowns.

Concept note-3: -Inventory management is the entire process of managing inventories from raw materials to finished products.

Concept note-4: -Inventory management is the planning and organization of a company’s inventory with the help of advanced and unique management systems.
