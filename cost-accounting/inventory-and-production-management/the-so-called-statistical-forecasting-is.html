The so-called statistical forecasting is

(A) ** Forecast material requirements based on past usage data

(B) Forecasting based on material usage information from material lifetime data

(C) Forecasting based on data from the Central Bureau of Statistics (BPS)

(D) Forecasting of predicted needs based on operational demand

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Forecasting – These are analyses applying statistics to historical data to project what could happen in the future.

Concept note-2: -Time-series forecasting is a data science technique that uses machine learning and other computer technologies to study past observations and predict future values of time-series data.

Concept note-3: -They are usually applied to intermediate-or long-range decisions. Examples of qualitative forecasting methods are informed opinion and judgment, the Delphi method, market research, and historical life-cycle analogy. Quantitative forecasting models are used to forecast future data as a function of past data.

Concept note-4: -Backtesting involves applying a strategy or predictive model to historical data to determine its accuracy. It allows traders to test trading strategies without the need to risk capital.
