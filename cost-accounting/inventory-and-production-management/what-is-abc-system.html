What is ABC system?

(A) ** A method of dividing on hand inventory into 3 classifications,

(B) A method of segregate the inventory into 3 classifications.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -ABC analysis is a method of inventory management that involves grouping inventory items into three categories, “A” items being the most important and “C” items being the least important.

Concept note-2: -ABC analysis is a method in which inventory is divided into three categories, i.e. A, B, and C in descending value. The items in the A category have the highest value, B category items are of lower value than A, and C category items have the lowest value. Inventory control and management are critical for a business.

Concept note-3: -ABC analysis divides an organization’s on-hand inventory into three classes based upon their value. This inventory categorization method divides items into A, B and C categories. A is the category of the most valuable items, with B and C ranking next.
