Which of the following would be suitable for using a job-order cost system?

(A) The production of personal computers.

(B) The production of automobiles.

(C) The refining of petroleum.

(D) ** The construction of a new campus building.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Answer and Explanation: The correct option is (D) the construction of a new campus building. Construction contracts are of special nature and every contract would be different from another.

Concept note-2: -Answer: d. The construction of a new campus building. In a job order costing, the costs are applied in each of the job.

Concept note-3: -Companies in the white-collar sector of business, including law firms, accounting businesses, and private investment companies, can utilize job order costing to manage individual client accounts. For example, accounting firms can consider each individual client a job.

Concept note-4: -Job order costing is used to measure the revenue gained against the expenses incurred during the production process so that we can determine the profit for every unique job which is being manufactured.

Concept note-5: -Software Services: Companies that build custom software for clients also use job order costing systems.
