The source documents that production managers use to request materials for production and which are used to assign the cost of materials to specific jobs or to overhead are:

(A) Job Cost Sheet

(B) Production Order

(C) ** Materials Requisition

(D) Materials Purchase Order

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct option is C. A material requisition, often referred to as a material request, is a document used by the manufacturing department to track the quantity of direct materials moved when raw materials were being used to produce goods or to seek the materials necessary to finish a production process.

Concept note-2: -Answer and Explanation: The correct answer is a. Materials requisition slip. Material requisition slip is a document used to record the used direct materials or amount of direct materials that were transferred from raw materials to work in process account during the period.

Concept note-3: -The source documents for direct materials is the invoices from the material purchases. The source documents for direct labor is the time cards of the employees. The source documents for the manufacturing overhead is the allocation method utilized by the firm in addition to the invoices of the overhead allocated.

Concept note-4: -Job cost sheet. This is the correct answer.
