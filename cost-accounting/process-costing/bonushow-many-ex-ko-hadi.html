EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Bonus Point In such a case, if the foot of the raider lands over the bonus line without cutting it, then the raiding team will receive a bonus (+1) point even if the raider is successfully tackled during the raid. Bonus points will not revive a player.

Concept note-2: -A bonus point is given only when there are 6 or more defenders or anti-raiders. The line closest to the end line is 1.75 m and is called the bonus line. A player has to cross the bonus line to get the bonus point with no contact on the surface towards the baulk line.

Concept note-3: -The baulk line is at a distance of 3.75m from the mid line while the bonus line is drawn 1m further back from the baulk line (between the baulk line and the end line).

Concept note-4: -The raider has to go to the opposing court with the continuous clear sound recitation aloud of the word ‘Kabaddi’ without stopping to take a breath and should try to touch an anti raider and make him out. The duration of a match for men shall be 2 halves of 20 minutes each.
