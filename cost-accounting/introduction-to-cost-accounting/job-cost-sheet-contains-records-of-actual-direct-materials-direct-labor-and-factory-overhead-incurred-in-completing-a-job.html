Job cost sheet contains records of actual direct materials, direct labor and factory overhead incurred in completing a job.

(A) TRUE

(B) ** FALSE

Concept note-1: -The job order cost sheet records details on the quantity of direct work and material and the sum of factory overhead charged to the particular job or job part.

Concept note-2: -Job cost sheets contain entries for actual direct material, actual direct labor, and actual manufacturing overhead cost incurred in completing a job.
