To control materials a business must maintain limited access, segregation of duties and accuracy in recording.

(A) ** TRUE

(B) FALSE

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -(A) The document authorizing the issuance of materials from the storeroom is a materials requisition. A materials requisition is a request to move materials from the warehouse to the production floor.

Concept note-2: -What is Material Control? Material Control is a management function that is concerned with the storage, handling, and use of materials to minimize waste and improve inventory accuracy. This process can be beneficial for companies to reduce costs and improve organization and productivity.

Concept note-3: -The purchasing agent is involved in the physical control of materials. Q. The purchasing agent is responsible for the preparation of material requisitions and specifying the kind of materials of materials needed.

Concept note-4: -It records receipts and issues of materials by the storekeeper as well as stock balances. Stores Ledger Account: It is a subsidiary Ledger maintained by the stores department to record the movement of stocks both in quantity and value.
