INTRODUCTION TO COST ACCOUNTING

[SOURCES]
A document that contains all information about the costs of a specific job is called a job order cost sheet.

(A) ** TRUE

(B) FALSE

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A job cost sheet is a document or record that compiles all of the costs that go into a particular job. It is a way for a company to separate costs. By separating the costs, a company can better understand how much they spent on each unit per product produced.

Concept note-2: -What is a Job Order Cost Sheet? A job order cost sheet accumulates the costs charged to a specific job. It is used within a job costing system. This cost sheet is most commonly compiled for single-unit or batch-sized production runs.

Concept note-3: -(also job order cost sheet) a list of the materials and labour costs for the production of a quantity of goods or of providing a particular service: Two of the final values on the job cost sheet are the total job cost and the profit.

Concept note-4: -Job-Cost Record. Source document that records and accumulates all the costs assigned to a specific job, starting when work begins. Also called job-cost sheet.
