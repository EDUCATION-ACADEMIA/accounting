“The amount of expenditure (actual or notional) incurred or attributable to a given thing” is

(A) expense

(B) ** cost

(C) revenue

(D) value

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -COST ACCOUNTING-AN INTRODUCTION. Cost means the amount of expenditure ( actual or notional) incurred on, or attributable to, a given thing.

Concept note-2: -According to the. terminology of British Institute of Cost and Works Accountants, ‘’Cost is the amount of expenditure (actual or notional) incurred on or attributable to a given thing”.

Concept note-3: -Answer: A unit cost is a total expenditure incurred by a company to produce, store, and sell one unit of a particular product or service.

Concept note-4: -In economics, an implicit cost, also called an imputed cost, implied cost, or notional cost, is the opportunity cost equal to what a firm must give up in order to use a factor of production for which it already owns and thus does not pay rent. It is the opposite of an explicit cost, which is borne directly.

Concept note-5: -Notional spending occurs when services or goods are given to a candidate for free or at a discount.
