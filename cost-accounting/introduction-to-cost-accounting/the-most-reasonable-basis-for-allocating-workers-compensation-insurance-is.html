Concept note-1: -For example, an employee got an injury in his ankle while unloading a truck of goods. This injury at the ankle caused him to limp and resultantly he was having back pain. Workers’ Compensation Insurance can help to cover his treatment of back pain because it was because of his ankle injury.

Concept note-3: -The Employee/Worker Compensation Policy helps the employer financially compensate employees who are injured or die because of an accident that happened while they were working.
