Total cost of a product:Rs. 10, 000 Profit:25% on Selling Price Profit is:

(A) Rs. 2, 500

(B) Rs. 3, 000

(C) ** Rs. 3, 333

(D) Rs. 2, 000

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Profit on S.P = 2500/(10000-2500) × 10000 = Rs. 3, 333.

Concept note-2: -% of Profit on selling price = (25 * 100)/125 = 20%

Concept note-3: -for ₹ 10, 000 and sell it at a profit of 20%. The money I will get for it is ₹ 12000.

Concept note-4: -Detailed Solution 100. ∴ The profit percentage is 33.33%.

Concept note-5: -Profit Percentage Formula This profit is based on the cost price, hence, the formula to find the profit percentage is: (Profit/Cost Price) × 100.
