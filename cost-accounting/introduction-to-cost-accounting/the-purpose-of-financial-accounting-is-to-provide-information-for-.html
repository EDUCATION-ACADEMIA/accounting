The purpose of financial accounting is to provide information for ____

(A) ** Assessing the profitability and financial position of the firm

(B) Fixing prices

(C) Locating factors leading to wastages and losses

(D) cost control

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The purpose of financial accounting is to provide information for assessing the profitability and financial position of the firm. The general purpose of the financial statements is to provide information about the results of operations, financial position, and cash flows of an organization.

Concept note-2: -The purpose of financial accounting is to prepare a companyâ€™s financial statements to reflect a specific period of time. The three most common varieties of financial statements are the balance sheet, income statement, and statement of cash flow.

Concept note-3: -Correct! The purpose of financial accounting is to provide useful information for outside investors, creditors, and others.

Concept note-4: -The objective of financial statements is to provide information about the financial position, performance and cash flows of an enterprise that is useful to a wide range of users in making economic decisions. 13. Financial statements prepared for this purpose meet the common needs of most users.
