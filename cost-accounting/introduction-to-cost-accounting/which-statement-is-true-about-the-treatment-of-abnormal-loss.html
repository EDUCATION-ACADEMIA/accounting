Which statement is true about the treatment of abnormal loss?

(A) Value is credited to process loss a/c

(B) Scrap value is credited to process loss a/c

(C) ** Balance in abnormal loss a/c is transferred to costing profit & loss a/c

(D) None of these

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Treatment of Abnormal Loss in Process Accounts If you use bad quality raw material in the production, there is big risk of wastage in production. So, use of bad quality raw material is the reason of abnormal loss. 2. Careless is also reason of abnormal loss.

Concept note-2: -Accounting for Abnormal Loss Abnormal loss causes monetary loss for the business and therefore be classified as an expense and presented in the profit and loss statement.

Concept note-3: -An abnormal loss is a cost to your business. It should therefore be treated as an expense and shown on your income statement.
