Weights for the respective materials are 10, 000, 6, 000 and 7, 500 pounds. What is the cost to be entered on the stock cards for each material, based on shipping weight?

(A) X 12.82; Y 12.82; Z 12.82

(B) X 12.81; Y 13.67; Z 13.67

(C) ** X 12.82; Y 12.82; Z 13.65

(D) X 12.81; Y 12.81; Z 13.67
