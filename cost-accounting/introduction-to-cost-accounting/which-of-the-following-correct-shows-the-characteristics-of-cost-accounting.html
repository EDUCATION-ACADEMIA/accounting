Which of the following CORRECT shows the characteristics of Cost Accounting?

(A) The financial statements describe the whole performance of the organization.

(B) The financial statements describe the whole performance of the organization.

(C) Report must follow Accounting Standards and Companies Act.

(D) ** No specific Accounting standards to be followed.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Characteristic Features of Cost Accounting It is a branch of accounting involving the cost of goods and services. Management is able to analyze the data which helps in decision-making and budgeting for the future. The data achieved is used in financial accounting.

Concept note-2: -Option D is the correct answer Process cost accounting system is mainly used by manufacturing organizations and therefore, as part of ongoing processes, stock of work in process is only partially completed at the start or end of an accounting period.

Concept note-3: -Under the Standard Costing System, which of the following actions is true about the cost variance? Explanation: The real cost of material cost, direct labor, and administration to produce a unit of product is known as actual cost. Variance is the difference between the actual and standard costs.

Concept note-4: -The chief characteristics of Standard Costing are the following:1. Cost determination: Standard Costing is designed to determine the cost of an output based on past experience and future trends. 2. Cost comparison: when actual costs are known, these are compared to budgeted costs.
